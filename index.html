Pokémon Aragonite

Pokémon Aragonite is a fanmade project based on the real-life island of Sicily, in the South of Italy. This project aims to reimagine Sicily into the Pokémon world by creating the Sylia region, where you will be able to explore many areas and cities based off actual places!

Initially, this started as a fakemon dex where we had fun interpreting our culture, flora, and fauna into Fakemon, but then we decided to use Pokémon Essentials to turn it into a playable game!

THE REGION
The colorful Sylia region offers a rich climate where many Pokémon have inhabited its ancient ruins and coastlines. The people live with the respect of its prominent volcano: Mount Ethenelle.

But it's not just sights that make Sylia unique, but also its rich and ancient history, myths of creatures inhabiting the Messen Strait, strange ruins on the island of Pharot, and the fascinating mosaics of Forfer Town...

Recently, Mt. Ethenelle seems to be acting more active than usual, and the law is getting involved in keeping an eye on suspicious activities, what could this mean? But there's a charismatic team of researchers that aims to study and uncover everything that is hidden on this island!

The Trainers

Embark on your journey as either Biagio or Cecilia alongside one of the three starters, it is gonna be quite a long trek! You aren't just going around to collect badges, but to become a fully fledged researcher!

As you collect Gym badges, ART will recognise you more and more as a proper member of their team, and you will be allowed to partake in their various expeditions.

You might help some people, or collect ancient relics, or perhaps find rare Pokémon!
